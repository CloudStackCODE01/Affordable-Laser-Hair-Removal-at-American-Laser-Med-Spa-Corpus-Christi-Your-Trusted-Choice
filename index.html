Frequently Asked Questions About Laser Hair Removal

  • Is laser hair removal safe for all skin types?

Our spa uses FDA-approved laser technology suitable for many skin tones. We customize settings to protect your skin and maximize effectiveness.

  • How many sessions will I need?

Most people require 6 to 8 sessions spaced several weeks apart. The exact number depends on your hair type and the area being treated.

  • Are there any side effects?

Some clients experience mild redness or swelling immediately after treatment, which usually subsides quickly. Our experienced technicians take every precaution to minimize risks.

  • How long do the results last?

Laser hair removal significantly reduces hair growth long-term. Some maintenance treatments might be needed over time depending on your body’s response.

  • Can I do laser hair removal on sensitive areas?

Yes, we safely treat delicate areas like the face, underarms, and bikini line with precision and care.
